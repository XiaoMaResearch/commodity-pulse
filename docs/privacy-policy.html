Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 24, 2026

Commodity Pulse displays commodity market information for oil, natural gas, gold, and silver. The app does not require account creation and does not intentionally collect personal information from users.

Information We Collect

Commodity Pulse does not collect or store personal data such as your name, email address, payment information, or precise location.

Data Used by the App

The app requests market quote and chart data from third-party financial data providers over the internet. The app also stores limited on-device preferences and cached market data, including favorites and the most recent quote snapshot, to improve app usability.

Analytics and Tracking

Commodity Pulse does not include advertising SDKs, third-party analytics SDKs, or cross-app tracking.

Data Sharing

Commodity Pulse does not sell personal data and does not share personal data with data brokers.

Data Retention

Cached quote data and user preferences remain on your device until removed by the app or deleted when the app is uninstalled.

Children's Privacy

Commodity Pulse is a general audience app and is not directed to children under 13.
